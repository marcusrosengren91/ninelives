About this game

Seagulls: Crappy Situation

Stranded at sea with weird seagulls closing in, all you've got is a slingshot, a water hose, and a raft that's barely holding together. Fight off endless waves, clean up the mess, and upgrade your way through the chaos solo or in local co-op. Expect rogue-lite mayhem, dumb humor, and some very questionable seagulls.

About Nine Lives

About Nine Lives Game Studio

Nine Lives Game Studio is an indie game studio from Karlstad, Sweden, founded by Victor Åhs Blomberg in 2020. Born from a love for creativity, games, and cats, we create titles with personality — funny, charming, a little weird, and built around ideas we genuinely want to play.

Our team of seven includes programmers, designers, artists, an animator, and a composer/sound designer, with backgrounds from Future Games, ChangeMaker, and Ingesund. We started our journey with From The Woods, a horror escape room inspired by Swedish mythology, which won a prize at the Summer Pitch event and gained early attention online.

In 2022, we shifted focus to Seagulls: Crappy Situation, a humor-driven game that quickly connected with players. The game won Best Demo at Summer Pitch 2024 and has been showcased at events including Nordic Game, Comic Con, GGX, and Gamescom.

As we continue to grow, Nine Lives Game Studio remains committed to making fun, memorable, and creative games for players around the world.
